{% trans "How We Use Your Information" %}

{% trans "Your information is used for the following purposes:" %}

  • {% trans "Processing and managing grant applications, awards, and related activities" %}

  • {% trans "Authorized reviewers assigned to evaluate grant applications" %}

{% trans "Certain information may also be subject to disclosure under applicable Freedom of Information laws. We will not sell or rent your personal information to third parties." %}

  • {% trans "Multi-factor authentication (MFA) support for enhanced account security" %}
  • {% trans "Comprehensive audit logging of all system activities" %}
  • {% trans "Regular security assessments and monitoring" %}

{% trans "Data Retention" %}

{% trans "Grant records and associated data are retained in accordance with the State Library's records retention schedules and applicable state and federal requirements. {% trans "Account information for users who have not participated in any grant activity may be removed after two (2) years of inactivity, following prior notification." %}
